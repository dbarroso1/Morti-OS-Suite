Morti-OS: EQE Module

Summary

The EQE Module is the part of Morti that calculates the weight of a conversation and outputs a emotional value. the Emotional Value (EV) comes from a Weight given to a word, the context of the scentence (weights of words surrounding it). Then Based on the weights of both the context and the word an EV can be estimated.
